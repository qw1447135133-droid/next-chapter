废弃医疗舱
A panoramic wide-angle view of a derelict, subterranean medical facility hidden beneath an industrial structure. The center of the composition features a sleek but grime-covered glass stasis pod, its reinforced shell cracked and leaking a faint, wispy cryo-gas. The surrounding architecture is a chilling blend of brutalist concrete walls and exposed high-tech machinery, with severed fiber-optic cables hanging like synthetic vines from the ceiling. Shattered glass shards and discarded surgical tubing litter the polished but dust-covered metal floor. The lighting is high-contrast and atmospheric: a harsh, pulsating crimson glow emanates from a nearby terminal screen displaying the digital countdown '72:00:00', while pale, sickly blue moonlight streaks down from a high, unreachable ventilation grate, cutting through the heavy, sterile haze of the room. The mood is one of clinical horror and claustrophobic isolation.

比弗利豪宅
A grand, multi-level architectural masterpiece nestled in the hills of Beverly Hills, showcasing the peak of Hollywood luxury and modern minimalism. The villa features a stark juxtaposition of floor-to-ceiling glass walls, polished charcoal concrete, and warm walnut wood accents. The panoramic composition captures the house from a slightly low angle, emphasizing its imposing stature against a sprawling Los Angeles skyline. An infinity pool sits in the foreground, its surface like glass, reflecting the geometric lines of the cantilevered balconies. The interior, visible through the transparent facade, reveals a cold, sterile opulence: a monolithic marble dining table under a custom sculptural chandelier, and a state-of-the-art study. Manicured tropical greenery and architectural uplighting create deep, dramatic shadows, while the overall atmosphere is one of clinical perfection masking a hidden, darker reality.
